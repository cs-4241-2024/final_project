Classmates Tracker

How to use

Use this to keep track of classmates and friends contact information that you meet throughout the years. To add a person enter their name, email, phone number, age seperated by commas and click submit. To delete a classmate, enter the persons full name into the delete bar, then click delete. To edit a classmate, enter their name, select what you want to edit, and enter the new value.
